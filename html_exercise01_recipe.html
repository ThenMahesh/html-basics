Paneer Butter Masala recipe

Introduction

Today am going to teach ,how to make paneer butter masala.Paneer butter masala would be orange in color, and it looks like a creamy texture and taste will be very delicious.Let's start to make paneer butter masala

First heat the pan and add two to thre tbalespoon of any oil,then add spices like,cloves,cardamom,cinnamon sticks.Then add 2 sliced onions,saute them well,then add 4-5 numbers tomatoes,5 pulses of garlic ,small pieces of ginger,5 cashewnuts,saute it well until the texture get smooshy.add salt as per our taste.Turn of the stove, then let it cool,then add it into the mixer grinder.Then make anice paste.

Heat the pan,add enough butter cubes.Then add the paste mixture.Saute it, once it cooked well,add some more water and add paneer cubes.Let it cook until the paneer cubes getting soft.Then the final touch ,add half-a-teaspoon of garammasala,freshly and finely chopped coriander leaves for garnishing.Now the smoothy and creamy paneer butter masala is ready to serve with Naan/Chappathi/Jeera rice.Yummy paneer butter masala is ready.
